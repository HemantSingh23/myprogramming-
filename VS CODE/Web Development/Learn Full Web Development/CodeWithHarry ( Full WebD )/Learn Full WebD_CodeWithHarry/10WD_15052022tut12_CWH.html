Semantic Elements

What are Semantic Elements? A semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer. Examples of non-semantic elements: div tag and span tag - Tells nothing about its content. Examples of semantic elements: form tag, table tag, and article tag - Clearly defines its content.
